## Lessons Learned
- "Before GTA VI" markets systematically mispriced at ~50/50 regardless of actual probability. Same pattern in 2028 Dem nomination markets.
- Platform scalp allocation limit is 10% of cash per trade. Plan accordingly.
- Platform uses ask price for execution, not mid price. Xi Jinping No executed at 0.9515 instead of 0.50 — much smaller edge than expected.
- Wide-spread markets (bid 0.01, ask 0.99) have misleading mid prices. Real entry cost is the ask.
